Browse Open Tasks — Find Paid Development Work

The Tasks page is your gateway to finding paid development work on PrTask. Every task listed here represents a real piece of software work posted by a client, complete with a bounty that you earn upon successful completion. Tasks span a wide range of complexity levels from trivial fixes that take minutes to expert-level implementations requiring deep domain knowledge. You can filter tasks by complexity level to find work that matches your skill set, and sort by newest or highest bounty to prioritize your search.

Each task card displays essential information at a glance: the complexity rating, bounty amount in PHP, a brief description, and relevant tags indicating the technologies involved. Complexity levels include Trivial, Simple, Medium, Complex, and Expert, with bounty amounts typically scaling with difficulty. Tags help you quickly identify tasks that match your technical expertise, whether that is C#, Blazor, Python, JavaScript, DevOps, or any other technology stack. Click on any task to view the full description, requirements, and linked GitHub repository.

Bounty amounts are determined by clients when they create a task and reflect the value of the work being requested. PrTask does not charge developers any fees — the bounty amount you see is the exact amount you receive upon approval. To get started, browse the available tasks, find one that matches your skills, review the requirements in the full task detail page, and submit your solution as a pull request. The entire workflow follows standard GitHub practices, making it accessible to any developer familiar with version control and code review processes.
