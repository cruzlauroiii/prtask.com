About PrTask

PrTask is a platform where developers earn money by submitting pull requests to real projects.

Our Mission

PrTask exists to make software development accessible, fair, and rewarding for everyone. We believe that talented developers everywhere deserve equal access to meaningful work and transparent compensation regardless of where they live.

What We Do

Clients post development tasks with bounties. Developers claim tasks, write code, and submit pull requests. Once a PR is reviewed, approved, and merged, the developer gets paid based on the complexity of their work.

Our Story

PrTask was founded with a simple idea: what if every code contribution had a clear, guaranteed bounty attached to it? Born from the frustration of unpaid open-source labor and opaque freelancing platforms, PrTask launched to create a marketplace where pull requests are the universal currency of work. From our first bounty to thousands of completed tasks, we have grown into a global community of developers and clients who share a commitment to transparent, merit-based collaboration.

Achievement Badges

Every merged PR earns achievement badges based on milestones. Unlock badges for your first PR, tenth PR, earnings milestones, and top leaderboard positions. Compete on the public leaderboard ranked by total earnings. All pull requests are public.

Secure Payments

Client bounty payments are processed via supported payment providers. When a client approves a pull request, they are automatically charged and the developer gets paid. No upfront deposit required. Developers choose their preferred payout method.

Core Values

Transparency

Every task, bounty, and payment is visible. Developers see exactly what they will earn before they start, and clients see exactly what they pay for. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Fairness

Compensation is tied directly to the work delivered. Code is reviewed on its merits, not on who wrote it. Every developer competes on a level playing field with equal opportunity.

Open Source

We champion open-source principles. All pull requests on PrTask are public, fostering knowledge sharing and community-driven quality standards across every project.

Community Impact

PrTask powers a thriving global community of developers and clients collaborating across borders and time zones to build better software together.

Open Source Commitment

PrTask is built on and for the open-source ecosystem. We contribute back to the community, sponsor open-source maintainers, and ensure that every line of code submitted through our platform remains accessible. Our review process is public, our leaderboard is public, and our commitment to openness is unwavering.

About PrTask — Our Mission and Values

PrTask was founded on the belief that software development talent exists everywhere, but opportunity does not. Our mission is to democratize access to paid development work by creating a transparent marketplace where skill and code quality are the only factors that matter. We built PrTask to eliminate the barriers that prevent talented developers in emerging markets from accessing well-paying software work, while simultaneously helping clients find skilled developers who can deliver production-ready code through a familiar pull request workflow.

Our core values of transparency, fairness, and open source commitment guide every decision we make. Transparency means all tasks, bounties, and reviews are publicly visible so both clients and developers can make informed decisions. Fairness means developers keep 100% of their bounty earnings with no hidden fees or deductions. Our commitment to open source means we actively support the ecosystem by enabling paid contributions to open source projects and maintaining our own platform code with community input.

PrTask is more than a marketplace — it is a community of developers and clients who share a vision of how software work should operate. We provide badges and XP to recognize achievements, maintain a leaderboard to highlight top contributors, and offer comprehensive payment options that work across borders. From our founding story as a project to empower Filipino developers to our growth into a global platform, PrTask remains committed to fair pay, quality code, and building trust between clients and developers through the universal language of pull requests.
