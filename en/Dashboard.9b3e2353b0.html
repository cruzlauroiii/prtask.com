Your PrTask Dashboard — Platform Overview and Activity Tracking

The Dashboard provides a centralized overview of platform activity on PrTask. At a glance, you can see the total number of active tasks available on the platform, the number of registered users, the total bounty pool across all open tasks, and the count of pull requests submitted. These statistics give you a real-time snapshot of the marketplace health and help you understand the current level of activity and opportunity available on the platform.

For developers, the Dashboard serves as a starting point to assess the current landscape of available work. When active tasks are listed, they appear directly on your dashboard with quick links to view details and start working. For clients, the Dashboard provides visibility into the status of tasks you have created, including any pending pull requests awaiting your review. The recent pull requests section shows the latest submissions across the platform, keeping you informed about ongoing development activity.

The Dashboard updates dynamically as new tasks are created, pull requests are submitted, and bounties are paid out. If you are a client looking to post new work, you can navigate directly to the task creation form from the Dashboard. If you are a developer looking for opportunities, the statistics help you gauge demand and competition. As the platform grows, the Dashboard will continue to serve as your command center for tracking all aspects of your PrTask activity, from earnings and submissions to task management and team collaboration.
