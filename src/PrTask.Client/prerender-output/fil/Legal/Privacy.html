Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with GitHub, we collect your GitHub username, display name, email address, and avatar URL. When you initiate or receive a payment, our payment provider processes payment details directly. Developers provide their preferred payout method details. We also collect task and pull request activity on the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate the PrTask platform, process bounty payments, display public leaderboard profiles and achievement badges, communicate with you about task status and payments, and prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information with our payment providers for payment processing and with your chosen payout provider for developer payouts. Your public profile (username, level, XP, badges) is visible on the leaderboard. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may disclose information if required by applicable law or a valid court order.

4. Payment Data

All client payment card data is handled exclusively by our payment provider. PrTask never stores credit card numbers or CVVs on our servers. Developer payout details are stored securely and shared only with the chosen payout provider. Transaction records (amount, status, date) are retained for accounting purposes.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. After account deletion, we retain transaction records for 5 years as required by applicable tax regulations. Anonymized usage statistics may be retained indefinitely.

6. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all communications, GitHub OAuth tokens with minimal required scopes, and industry-standard security practices. However, no system is perfectly secure. Report vulnerabilities to security at prtask.com.

7. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access your personal data held by PrTask, correct inaccurate or incomplete data, request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements), and object to processing of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

PrTask is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us for removal.

9. International Data Transfers

PrTask servers are hosted via Cloudflare. Your data may be processed in multiple jurisdictions. We ensure adequate data protection measures are in place for any such transfers.

10. Contact

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your data rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy at prtask.com.
