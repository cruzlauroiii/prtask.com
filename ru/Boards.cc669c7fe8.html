Kanban Boards — Visual Task Management and Workflow

Boards on PrTask provide a visual, column-based approach to managing development tasks using the Kanban methodology. Each board displays tasks organized into workflow stages — typically columns like To Do, In Progress, In Review, and Done — giving you an at-a-glance view of where every task stands in the development pipeline. This visual representation makes it easy to identify bottlenecks, balance workloads, and ensure that tasks flow smoothly from creation through completion and payment.

Creating a board is simple: provide a name and select the board type (Kanban is the default). Once created, you can add tasks to the board and organize them across columns to reflect their current status. Boards support drag-and-drop interaction for moving tasks between stages, making workflow management intuitive and fast. You can create multiple boards for different projects, repositories, or teams, keeping your work organized in ways that make sense for your specific development workflow and organizational structure.

PrTask boards sync in real time via WebSocket connections, so changes made by any team member are immediately visible to everyone viewing the board. This real-time collaboration is essential for distributed teams where developers may be working across different time zones. Boards integrate with the PrTask task system, so when a developer submits a pull request for a task on your board, the task status updates automatically. Combined with sprint management and the leaderboard, boards complete the project management toolkit that PrTask provides, enabling teams to plan, execute, track, and celebrate their development work in one unified platform.
