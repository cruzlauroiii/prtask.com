Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Automatic Payment Model

PrTask uses an automatic payment model. Clients are only charged when they approve a pull request. No upfront payment or deposit is required when posting a task.

2. No Charge Before Approval

Clients are not charged until they explicitly approve a pull request. If no PR is approved, no payment is made. Clients may cancel a task at any time before approving a PR at no cost.

3. Refund After Approval

Once a client approves a pull request and is charged, the bounty is paid to the developer. Refunds are only issued in cases of platform error, fraudulent activity, or if the submitted work is materially different from what was described in the PR.

4. Disputed Pull Requests

If a client believes an approved PR does not meet the task requirements, PrTask initiates an arbitration process. Both parties must submit evidence within 7 business days. Possible outcomes include full refund to client if work does not meet requirements, full payment to developer if work meets the stated task requirements, or partial refund if work partially meets requirements (split determined by PrTask).

5. Abandoned Tasks

If a task receives no pull request submissions, the client may close it at any time. Since no payment is made until PR approval, there is nothing to refund for abandoned tasks.

6. Platform Errors

If a payment is processed incorrectly due to a platform error, PrTask will issue a full refund within 3 business days. Contact support at prtask.com with your transaction reference number.

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, log in to your PrTask account, navigate to the task in question, and select "Request Refund." Alternatively, email support at prtask.com with your task ID and reason for the refund request. All refund requests are reviewed within 2 business days.

8. Refund Method

Refunds are processed to the original payment method used. Processing times depend on your bank or card issuer but typically take 5-7 business days after approval.

9. Chargeback Policy

If a client initiates a chargeback through their bank or credit card issuer instead of using our dispute resolution process, PrTask reserves the right to suspend the client's account pending investigation. Clients who file chargebacks without first attempting resolution through PrTask may have their accounts permanently terminated. We encourage all clients to use our built-in dispute and refund process before contacting their financial institution, as chargebacks incur fees and delays for all parties involved.

10. No Escrow Model

PrTask does not use an escrow system. No funds are held in advance. Payment is triggered solely by the client's approval of a pull request. This means clients retain full control of their funds until they actively choose to approve work. Developers should understand that payment is contingent on client approval and is not guaranteed upon PR submission.
