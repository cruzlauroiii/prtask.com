Presyo

Ang PrTask ay ganap na libre para sa lahat. Walang mga nakatagong bayarin, walang komisyon, walang singil.

Para sa mga Developer

Libre

  • Matanggap ang 100% ng bounty sa bawat nakumpletong PR
  • Kumita ng mga badge at ranggo sa leaderboard

  • Mabayaran sa pamamagitan ng iyong gustong paraan ng payout

Libre

  • Suporta sa dispute resolution at arbitrasyon

Mga Detalye ng Bayad

How are payments processed?

When a client approves a pull request, they are automatically charged the bounty amount via their payment method. The developer receives 100% of the bounty.

When do I get paid?

Payment is processed automatically when your PR is approved by the client. Developers receive 100% of the bounty via their preferred payout method.

What currencies are supported?

All transactions are in Philippine Pesos (PHP).

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and other major credit and debit cards through our secure payment processor.

How long until I receive my payout?

Payouts are typically processed within 3 to 5 business days after the client approves your pull request.

Is there a minimum bounty amount?

Yes, the minimum bounty that a client can set for a task is ₱1. There is no maximum limit.

Handa Ka Na Bang Magsimula?

Sumali sa PrTask ngayon at magsimulang kumita o mag-post ng mga bounty. Ito ay ganap na libre.

PrTask Pricing — Transparent and Developer-Friendly

PrTask operates on a fundamentally developer-friendly pricing model: developers pay nothing and keep 100% of every bounty they earn. There are no subscription fees, no commission percentages, and no hidden charges deducted from developer payouts. When a client posts a task with a ₱500 bounty, the developer who completes and gets their pull request approved receives the full ₱500. This pricing structure reflects our belief that the people writing the code should receive the full value of their work.

For clients, PrTask is also free to use. Clients pay only the bounty amount they set when creating tasks, plus standard payment processing fees charged by the payment gateway (credit card, bank transfer, or mobile wallet). There are no platform subscription fees, no markup on bounties, and no ongoing costs. Enterprise clients who need custom integrations, dedicated support, or volume task management can contact us for tailored solutions. Our pricing transparency extends to the feature comparison table above, which confirms that all core features are included for both developers and clients at no cost.

Payment processing on PrTask supports multiple methods to accommodate users worldwide. Clients can fund bounties using credit cards, bank transfers, GCash, GrabPay, Maya, and QR Ph payments. Developer payouts are processed automatically when a pull request is approved, with disbursement available via bank transfer or PayPal. The minimum bounty amount ensures meaningful compensation for development work, and all transactions are processed securely through PCI DSS compliant payment infrastructure. Review our refund policy for details on how payments are handled in case of disputes or task cancellations.
