О PrTask

PrTask — это платформа, где разработчики зарабатывают деньги, отправляя pull request в реальные проекты.

Наша миссия

PrTask существует, чтобы сделать разработку программного обеспечения доступной, справедливой и вознаграждающей для всех. Мы верим, что талантливые разработчики по всему миру заслуживают равного доступа к значимой работе и прозрачной оплате, независимо от того, где они живут.

Что мы делаем

Клиенты публикуют задачи на разработку с вознаграждениями. Разработчики берут задачи, пишут код и отправляют pull request. После того как PR проверен, одобрен и объединён, разработчик получает оплату в зависимости от сложности работы.

Our Story

PrTask was founded with a simple idea: what if every code contribution had a clear, guaranteed bounty attached to it? Born from the frustration of unpaid open-source labor and opaque freelancing platforms, PrTask launched to create a marketplace where pull requests are the universal currency of work. From our first bounty to thousands of completed tasks, we have grown into a global community of developers and clients who share a commitment to transparent, merit-based collaboration.

Achievement Badges

Every merged PR earns achievement badges based on milestones. Unlock badges for your first PR, tenth PR, earnings milestones, and top leaderboard positions. Compete on the public leaderboard ranked by total earnings. All pull requests are public.

Secure Payments

Client bounty payments are processed via supported payment providers. When a client approves a pull request, they are automatically charged and the developer gets paid. No upfront deposit required. Developers choose their preferred payout method.

Основные ценности

Transparency

Every task, bounty, and payment is visible. Developers see exactly what they will earn before they start, and clients see exactly what they pay for. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Fairness

Compensation is tied directly to the work delivered. Code is reviewed on its merits, not on who wrote it. Every developer competes on a level playing field with equal opportunity.

Open Source

We champion open-source principles. All pull requests on PrTask are public, fostering knowledge sharing and community-driven quality standards across every project.

Community Impact

PrTask powers a thriving global community of developers and clients collaborating across borders and time zones to build better software together.

Open Source Commitment

PrTask is built on and for the open-source ecosystem. We contribute back to the community, sponsor open-source maintainers, and ensure that every line of code submitted through our platform remains accessible. Our review process is public, our leaderboard is public, and our commitment to openness is unwavering.
