Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and improve your experience. PrTask uses cookies as described below.

2. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for the platform to function correctly. These include session cookies that maintain your authenticated state, security cookies that protect against cross-site request forgery, and load-balancing cookies. You cannot opt out of essential cookies, as they are necessary for core functionality.

3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember your preferences such as theme selection (dark or light mode), language settings, and notification preferences. These cookies enhance your experience but are not strictly required for the platform to operate.

4. Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with PrTask, including page views, feature usage, and performance metrics. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized. No personal data is shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Certain third-party services integrated with PrTask may set their own cookies. These include our payment processor for secure transaction handling and GitHub for OAuth authentication. Third-party cookies are governed by the respective provider's cookie and privacy policies. PrTask does not control these cookies.

7. Cookie Consent

When you first visit PrTask, a cookie consent banner informs you about our use of cookies and allows you to accept all, reject all, or customize your preferences. Essential cookies are always active and cannot be disabled. Analytics and marketing cookies require your opt-in consent. You may update your cookie preferences at any time through your account settings or by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

8. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, view stored cookies, and set preferences per site. Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using PrTask. For detailed instructions, consult your browser's help documentation.
